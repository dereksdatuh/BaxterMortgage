## 5. Myth-Busting Short Video (Reels/TikTok, ~60 sec)

**Script template:**
- Hook (0-3 sec): "If you think you need [X]% down to buy a house in
  Maine, watch this."
- Myth (3-15 sec): State the common misconception.
- Truth (15-40 sec): Explain the real numbers/program, using a simple
  example (e.g., "$250,000 home with 3.5% down = $8,750").
- CTA (40-60 sec): "Send me a message and I'll run your numbers for free —
  no pressure, no obligation."

**Myth ideas (rotate):**
1. "You need perfect credit to get a mortgage."
2. "Pre-qualification and pre-approval are the same thing."
3. "Renting is always cheaper than buying."
4. "You can't buy a home if you're self-employed."
5. "USDA loans are only for farms."
6. "You should wait until rates drop to start the process."
7. "Closing costs are the same everywhere."
