## 7. Tuesday 6/23 — Credit Tips Before You Apply (07-tue-0623-credit.png)

3 things to do (and NOT do) before you apply for a mortgage. 📋

1️⃣ Leave your credit cards alone. Don't open new accounts, don't close old ones, just let things sit as they are.

2️⃣ Hold off on big purchases. A new car, furniture on a store card, anything that adds debt before closing can change what you qualify for.

3️⃣ Keep paying everything on time. Even small bills like a phone or streaming subscription matter more than people think.

The short version: once you start the process, try to keep your financial picture as "boring" and stable as possible until after closing.

Already worried about something on your credit, or wondering how it might affect your options? Let's talk about it before you start house hunting, not after.
