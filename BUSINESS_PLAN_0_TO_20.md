### Key Market Characteristics

**Buyer Segments in Your Area:**
- **First-time buyers** (Biddeford, Saco, Arundel, Wells) -- priced out of Portland, seeking $300K-$450K
- **Move-up families** (Kennebunk, Wells, Scarborough) -- $450K-$700K
- **Luxury/second-home buyers** (Kennebunkport, Ogunquit, Cape Porpoise) -- $700K-$2M+
- **Vacation rental investors** -- seeking cash-flow properties near coast
- **Retirees downsizing** -- equity-rich, looking for smaller coastal homes

**Seasonal Patterns:**
- **Peak (April-September):** Tourism season drives listing activity; highest volume of transactions. Summer buyers include second-home purchasers and out-of-state relocators
- **Shoulder (March, October):** Serious buyers with less competition; great time to market to move-up buyers
- **Winter (November-February):** Lowest inventory, fewest transactions. Best time for relationship building, agent partnerships, and planning. Prices remain stable due to low supply

### Demographic Opportunity

Southern Maine is seeing net in-migration from Greater Boston, with remote workers and retirees seeking coastal lifestyle at 40-60% lower prices than coastal Massachusetts. This creates a unique opportunity for a broker who can guide out-of-state buyers through Maine-specific programs and processes.
