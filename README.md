## 90-Day Quick Start

**Week 1-2: Foundation**
1. Read `branding/brand-guide.md`, update your email signature.
2. Set up `client-pipeline/pipeline-tracker.csv` in Google Sheets/Excel.
3. Build your initial referral partner list in `referral-partners/partner-tracker.csv` — aim for 25 names (Realtors, attorneys, CPAs, financial planners, builders, insurance agents).

**Week 3-4: Outreach Launch**
4. Send the "reconnect/introduction" email from `referral-partners/realtor-outreach-templates.md` to your full list.
5. Schedule 5 coffee/lunch meetings per week with top-tier partners using the meeting scripts.
6. Start posting 3x/week using `content-marketing/social-post-templates.md`.

**Month 2: Systematize**
7. Launch the monthly client/partner newsletter (`content-marketing/email-newsletter-templates.md`).
8. Set up automated follow-up sequences for every new lead (`client-pipeline/follow-up-sequences.md`).
9. Pick one community event/sponsorship from `events-community/` and commit to it.

**Month 3: Compound**
10. Ask every closed client for a Google review + 2 referral names using `client-pipeline/review-referral-request-templates.md`.
10b. Set up the lead capture form + Google Sheet from `automation/zapier-lead-pipeline-blueprint.md` (Zap 1) so new leads get an instant response.
11. Host or co-host a first-time homebuyer seminar with a Realtor partner
    (see `events-community/event-and-sponsorship-ideas.md`).
12. Review your `partner-tracker.csv` — drop dead relationships, double down on active referral sources (Pareto: 20% of partners = 80% of referrals).

## The Core Philosophy

Top producers in Maine win on **relationships + responsiveness + local
expertise**, not big ad budgets. This toolkit is built around three flywheels:

1. **Referral Partners** → Realtors, attorneys, builders, CPAs, financial
   planners send you borrowers because you make them look good and you're
   fast.
2. **Past Clients** → Every closed loan becomes a source of 2+ referrals and
   a repeat customer (refi, HELOC, next home) if you stay in touch.
3. **Local Visibility** → Consistent, helpful content + community presence
   makes you the loan officer people already "know" before they call.

Work all three every week, every month, forever. That's how you become a
top producer in Maine.
