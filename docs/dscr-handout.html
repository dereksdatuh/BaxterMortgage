DSCR INVESTOR LOANS

Qualify Using the Property's Income, Not Yours

The Big Idea

A DSCR loan lets real estate investors qualify based on the rental income of the property rather than personal income. No tax returns. No W-2s. No pay stubs. If the property's rent covers the payment, you could qualify.

How It Works
Monthly Rent ÷ Monthly Payment = DSCR
If your DSCR is 1.00 or above, the property's income covers the mortgage. Some programs go as low as 0.80.
Quick Specs

Minimums

  • Credit Score: as low as 640
  • Down Payment: as low as 20%
  • DSCR Ratio: as low as 0.80 (some programs have no minimum)
  • Reserves: 3-6 months

Options

  • Term: 30-year and 40-year fixed
  • Interest Only: available
  • Max Loan: up to $2M+
  • Short-Term Rentals: yes, select programs
