## Phone/Coffee Meeting Talking Points (First Meeting)

1. **Ask first, pitch second.** "Tell me about your business — what's
   working, what's frustrating you with lenders right now?"
2. **Identify their pain point** (slow lenders, bad communication, deals
   falling through at the last minute, buyers who don't actually qualify).
3. **Position yourself as the fix**, using your specifics: direct cell
   access, same-day pre-approvals, attend closings, knowledge of
   USDA/rural/Maine-specific quirks.
4. **Make a concrete next step**: "Send me your next pre-approval request
   and let's see how it goes" or "Let's do a lunch-and-learn with your
   team next month."
5. **Follow up within 24 hours** with a short thank-you note/text.
