# How to Use `partner-tracker.csv`

Import this CSV into Google Sheets or Excel. It's your referral partner CRM.

## Relationship Stages
1. **New Contact** — Met once or got a name/intro, no follow-up yet.
2. **Warming** — Had a coffee/call, exchanged info, on each other's radar.
3. **Active** — Actively sending each other referrals occasionally.
4. **Core Partner** — Top 10-15 relationships. Regular communication,
   consistent two-way referrals, co-marketing.
5. **Dormant** — Was active, has gone quiet (6+ months no contact) —
   re-engage or let go.

## Who Goes On This List (Categories)
- **Realtors** (your #1 source — target 30-50 relationships, 10-15 "core")
- **Real Estate Attorneys** (closing-side referrals)
- **CPAs / Tax Preparers** (self-employed clients needing bank statement
  loans, refi clients with equity questions)
- **Financial Planners / Wealth Advisors** (clients buying second
  homes/investment property)
- **Builders / Contractors / Remodelers** (construction & renovation loans,
  new construction buyers)
- **Insurance Agents** (every new homeowner needs homeowners insurance —
  natural two-way referral)
- **Divorce Attorneys / Mediators** (one party often needs to refinance to
  buy out the other)
- **Property Managers** (investor clients, DSCR loan candidates)

## Weekly Habit
Every Monday, spend 30 minutes:
1. Sort by "Next Action Date" — anything due this week, do it.
2. Add 2-3 new names to the list (every new contact you meet).
3. Move at least 1 person from "Warming" to "Active" by sending a referral
   their way, inviting them to something, or sharing a useful resource.

## Target Cadence by Stage
| Stage | Contact Frequency |
|---|---|
| Core Partner | Every 2-3 weeks (call, drop-by, lunch, or referral) |
| Active | Monthly |
| Warming | Every 6-8 weeks |
| New Contact | Within 3 days of meeting, then follow the "New Partner Sequence" below |
