# Community Visibility & Events — Maine

Being a known, trusted face in your community drives referrals as much as
direct outreach. Pick 1-2 of these per quarter and commit fully rather than
spreading thin across many.

## Co-Hosted Events with Referral Partners
- **First-Time Homebuyer Seminar** — Partner with a Realtor (and ideally a
  real estate attorney + insurance agent) to host a free evening seminar at
  a library, community center, or brewery. Cover: budgeting, pre-approval,
  the offer process, closing costs. Capture attendee contact info for your
  newsletter.
- **"Lunch and Learn" at Realtor Offices** — See script in
  `referral-partners/realtor-outreach-templates.md` (#5).
- **Open House Drop-Ins** — Show up at a partner Realtor's open house with
  flyers/business cards and offer free on-the-spot pre-qualification chats
  for visitors.

## Local Sponsorships (high visibility, builds "known name" recognition)
- Little League / youth sports team sponsorship (logo on jerseys/banners)
- Local 5K/road race sponsorship
- Town festival or fair booth (many Maine towns have summer festivals —
  great for face time)
- Food pantry / community organization donation drives (e.g., "Bring 3
  cans, get a free [item]" at an event booth)
- Chamber of Commerce membership + ribbon cuttings/mixers

## Chamber of Commerce & Networking Groups
- Join your local Chamber of Commerce — attend mixers monthly, volunteer
  for a committee (visibility + relationship-building)
- Consider BNI (Business Network International) or similar structured
  referral groups — look for a chapter that doesn't already have a loan
  officer
- Real estate investor meetups (Maine has active investor communities —
  great fit for your DSCR/investor loan programs)

## Educational/Content Partnerships
- Offer to write a recurring "Ask the Lender" column for a local Realtor's
  newsletter or a community Facebook group
- Partner with a financial planner for a joint "Buying Your First Home /
  Planning for It Financially" workshop

## Holiday/Seasonal Touches
- Send handwritten holiday cards to your top 25-50 referral partners and
  past clients (stands out in a digital world)
- "Thanksgiving thank-you" calls/texts to your core referral partners —
  genuine, no ask attached

## Tracking
Add a row to `referral-partners/partner-tracker.csv` for every new contact
made at an event within 48 hours — momentum dies fast if contacts aren't
captured immediately.
