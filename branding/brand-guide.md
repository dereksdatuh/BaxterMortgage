# Baxter Mortgage Brand Guide (Derek Smith Edition)

## Logo & Tagline
- Logo: "BAXTER" in navy, with a green gradient "X", green underline, and
  "mortgage" in green script beneath.
- Tagline: **"We'll Guide You Home"**

## Color Palette

| Use | Color | Approx. Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Primary (headlines, logo text "BAXTER") | Navy Blue | `#1B3A5C` |
| Secondary/Accent (X mark, highlights) | Light Green | `#7AC97A` |
| Secondary/Accent (X mark gradient, underline, "mortgage" script) | Deep Teal Green | `#1E9B6E` |
| Background/Neutral | White | `#FFFFFF` |
| Text/Body | Charcoal Gray | `#333333` |

**Usage rule of thumb:** Navy for headers/text, green for accents, CTAs, and
underlines/dividers. Keep backgrounds white/light gray for a clean,
trustworthy financial look. Avoid red/orange — they clash with the brand
and can feel "alarm"-like for financial content.

## Voice & Tone
- **Warm but expert.** You're the knowledgeable friend, not a salesperson.
- **Maine-proud.** Reference local towns, seasons, and the housing market
  honestly (camps, four-season living, "from Kittery to Fort Kent").
- **Plain language.** Explain mortgage jargon in one sentence before using
  acronyms (e.g., "DSCR loan — qualify based on the property's rental
  income, not your personal income").
- **Action-oriented.** Every piece of content ends with a clear next step
  (call, text, schedule a call, download a guide).

## Standard Mortgage Disclosure Language (use on all marketing)

Short version (social posts, flyers):
> Baxter Mortgage, NMLS# 2752768. Derek Smith, NMLS# 2810853. Equal Housing
> Lender. Not a commitment to lend.

Long version (emails, newsletters, printed materials):
> Baxter Mortgage (NMLS# 2752768) | Derek Smith, Loan Officer, NMLS#
> 2810853. Equal Housing Opportunity. All loans subject to credit approval,
> income verification, and property appraisal. Rates and programs subject
> to change without notice. This is not an offer to extend credit or a
> commitment to lend. Terms and conditions apply.

## Required Compliance Logo
Every flyer, print piece, and paid ad must include the **Equal Housing
Opportunity** logo (the house + equal sign icon) alongside the NMLS
disclosure text. Place it in a footer/corner, sized legibly (at least
~0.5in / 40px). Save the official logo file to
`branding/assets/equal-housing-logo.png` and reuse it in every Canva
template, flyer, and ad creative.

## Photo/Graphic Guidelines
- Use real Maine imagery where possible: coastal scenes, classic New
  England homes, fall foliage, downtown Main Streets (Portland, Bangor,
  Bar Harbor, Camden, Augusta).
- Keep your headshot consistent across all platforms (LinkedIn, Facebook,
  Google Business Profile, Zillow, email signature).
- Always pair the Baxter logo with the "Equal Housing Lender" / NMLS
  disclosure on any printed flyer or paid social graphic.
