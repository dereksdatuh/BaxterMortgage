# Content Batching SOP (Canva + Higgsfield)

Goal: turn ~2 hours every 4 weeks into a full month of social content,
instead of scrambling daily. This SOP works directly off
`content-marketing/social-media-calendar-template.csv` and
`content-marketing/social-post-templates.md`.

---

## Step 1: Plan (15 min)
1. Open `content-marketing/social-media-calendar-template.csv` in Sheets.
2. Fill in the actual topic for each row (e.g., which loan program, which
   Maine town, which myth).
3. Note which posts need: (a) a static graphic, (b) a short video/Reel, or
   (c) just text + stock/Maine photo.

## Step 2: Build a Canva Template Set (one-time, ~30 min)
Create reusable Canva templates so each batch is just swapping text/images:
1. **"Loan Program Spotlight" template** — Baxter navy/green branding,
   headline area, body text area, NMLS disclosure footer (pre-filled, never
   delete: "NMLS# 2810853 | Company NMLS# 2752768 | Equal Housing Lender")
2. **"Town Spotlight" template** — photo background area + town name +
   caption overlay
3. **"Client Win / Closing" template** — celebratory design, "🎉🔑 SOLD"
   style, disclosure footer
4. **"Myth vs. Reality" template** — split layout (Myth / Reality), good
   for carousel or Reel cover
5. **"Quote/Tip" template** — simple branded card for seasonal tips

Save each as a Canva **Template** (Canva → "..." → Save as template) so you
can duplicate and edit quickly each month.

## Step 3: Batch-Generate Graphics (Canva)
For each week's posts in the calendar:
1. Duplicate the matching template
2. Swap headline/body text using the content from
   `social-post-templates.md`
3. Swap photo (use Maine-specific stock or your own photos — see note
   below on sourcing)
4. Export as PNG/JPG, save to a "Month X Content" folder in Canva or Drive

## Step 4: Batch-Generate Video Content (Higgsfield)
For Reels/TikTok myth-busting or process-explainer scripts (templates #5
and #8 in `social-post-templates.md`):
1. Use Higgsfield's `generate_video` tool with a short script based on the
   template (hook → myth → truth → CTA structure)
2. Keep videos 30-60 seconds
3. Add captions/text overlay matching your brand colors (navy/green)
4. Export and add to your content folder

> If you have your own photos/footage (recommended — authenticity beats
> stock), use Higgsfield's media upload to incorporate your own clips/
> photos into edited content.

## Step 5: Schedule Everything (Metricool/Later/Buffer)
1. Upload the month's batch of graphics/videos to your scheduler
2. Assign each to its calendar slot (day/platform) from the CSV
3. Add captions (copy directly from `social-post-templates.md`, personalize
   placeholders)
4. Add hashtags from the hashtag bank in `social-post-templates.md`
5. Review the whole month, then schedule — done.

## Step 6: Engagement Pass (5-10 min/day, not batchable)
Batching content does NOT mean batching engagement. Each day:
- Respond to comments/DMs within a few hours
- Spend 5-10 minutes engaging with Realtor partners' posts (likes,
  thoughtful comments) — this is relationship-building, not just
  visibility

---

## Sourcing Real Maine Photos (Better Than Stock)
- Take your own photos: closings, drives around your service area, local
  landmarks, your office/workspace
- Ask Realtor partners if you can use photos from closed listings
  (with permission) for "Town Spotlight" posts
- Build a simple photo library (Google Drive folder organized by town/
  season) — add to it constantly, draw from it during batching

## Monthly Batching Checklist
- [ ] Fill in calendar CSV with this month's topics
- [ ] Duplicate/edit Canva templates for each post
- [ ] Generate 1-2 short videos via Higgsfield
- [ ] Write/personalize captions from template bank
- [ ] Schedule full month in Metricool/Later
- [ ] Block 10 min/day on calendar for engagement (non-negotiable)
