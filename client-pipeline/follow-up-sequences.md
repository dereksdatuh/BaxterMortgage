# Client Pipeline Stages & Follow-Up Sequences

## Pipeline Stages (for `pipeline-tracker.csv`)
1. **New Lead** — Inquired, not yet contacted
2. **Contacted** — Initial conversation done
3. **Application** — Application started/in progress
4. **Pre-Approved** — Pre-approval letter issued
5. **Under Contract** — Purchase agreement signed, in processing
6. **Clear to Close** — Underwriting complete
7. **Closed** — Funded/closed
8. **Past Client** — Move to long-term nurture (newsletter +
   annual check-ins, see `content-marketing/email-newsletter-templates.md`)
9. **Lost/Dead** — Didn't proceed (still add to a "dormant" nurture list —
   circumstances change)

## Response Time Standard
- **New leads:** Contact within 1 hour during business hours, by end of day
  otherwise. Speed is the #1 differentiator for Realtor referrals.
- **Active clients (under contract):** Same-day response to any
  call/text/email. Set expectations up front ("I usually respond within a
  few hours, faster on weekdays").

## New Lead Follow-Up Cadence (if no response)
| Touch | Timing | Channel | Message |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Immediate | Call + Text | "Hi [Name], this is Derek from Baxter Mortgage — got your info from [source]. Do you have 10 min today or tomorrow to chat about your home loan options?" |
| 2 | +1 day | Email | Send the "New Lead Nurture" email from `content-marketing/email-newsletter-templates.md` |
| 3 | +3 days | Text | "Hey [Name], just following up — no pressure at all. Happy to answer questions whenever works for you." |
| 4 | +7 days | Email | Send a useful resource (loan program guide, first-time buyer checklist) |
| 5 | Ongoing | Newsletter | Add to monthly newsletter list permanently |

## Active Loan (Under Contract) Communication Checklist
- [ ] Day of application: Send welcome email with what to expect + document
  checklist
- [ ] Weekly: Proactive status update (even if "no change yet, just
  confirming we're on track for [date]")
- [ ] When conditions are issued: Same-day request to client, with a plain-
  English explanation of *why* each item is needed
- [ ] Clear to close: Notify client + Realtor + attorney same day,
  including closing disclosure timeline (3-day rule)
- [ ] Day before closing: Confirm time/location/funds needed with client

## Realtor/Attorney Communication During Active Loans
- Add Realtor and attorney to a CC group or shared update thread (with
  client permission) so they're not chasing you for status — this is a
  major referral-relationship builder.
- Send a brief weekly "loan status" update to the Realtor on every active
  file: Application → Processing → Underwriting → Clear to Close → Closed.

## Past Client Long-Term Nurture (after closing)
See `content-marketing/email-newsletter-templates.md` for the "Just Closed"
drip and annual mortgage check-up. Combine with:
- Birthday/home purchase anniversary card or text
- Holiday card (physical mail stands out)
- Annual "home equity check-in" — especially valuable in a rising market
